Soundscapes

Field recordings, ambient sounds and other curiosities. Feel free to download and use them!

I really enjoy using field recordings in my music, mainly nature and ambient sounds. I feel they sometimes "melt" with the music itself in a very natural way. I've started to collect some of the sounds that interest me, mainly for fun and to use in my music if suitable.

Please beware, if you listen through, freesound's streaming bandwidth introduces some glitches and reduces audio quality significantly for some of the recordings. If you wish to listen to the sounds in their best quality please download them directly from my freesound account.

So, not being a project in itself, this section is more like a compromise with myself to stop using other people's field-recordings and instead start making and using my own! :)
