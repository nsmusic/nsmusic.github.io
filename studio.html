REMOTE RECORDING

Nuno has been regularly collaborating with producers and musicians from all over the world, offering remote recording sessions from his studio in downtown Porto, Portugal. He has extensive experience in adapting the different techniques and the exotic sound of traditional instruments to different and modern musical contexts, such as movie, documentary and game soundtracks, singer songwriter, world/folk music, edm, etc.

Most of his ethnic string instruments are available for recording, feel free to check them out in the instrument page.

Recording fees are standard, they vary according to the recording duration and demands of each project. I can work with the most detailed scores or with free improvisations on a given source material, depending on the project's demands. If you are interested in a collaboration, please drop me a line here or send me an email describing your project.
